## 🧠 Press Bio (Full)

Mark Majcherczyk-Olczak is an independent multi-disciplinary creator working across music, film, and visual effects. As an artist and producer, his music blends experimental ideas with the familiar clarity of traditional pop, combining bright melodies, textured production, and modern rhythms.

His catalogue is beginning to gain international traction, with listeners across the UK, Canada, South Africa, and India. His release *Glidin' in the Club* has surpassed 3,000 streams, contributing to over 4,200 total streams across his Spotify catalogue.

Alongside his music career, Mark is the founder of **Majcherczyk Digital Ltd**, a production company developing film and digital media projects. He is currently leading the development of *#NOTHER*, a feature film exploring identity and social media culture.

In visual effects, Mark specialises in compositing using Nuke, focusing on integrating live-action footage with digital elements to create cinematic results.

With a growing audience of over 23.7K followers on Instagram and over 7.2 million total views across content, Mark continues to build a cross-platform presence as both a creative and entrepreneur.

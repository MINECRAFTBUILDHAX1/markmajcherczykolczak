# #NOTHER – Feature Film

#NOTHER is an independent coming-of-age feature film exploring identity, authenticity, and the impact of social media culture on modern teenage life.

---

## 🎥 Overview

**Title:** #NOTHER  
**Format:** Feature Film (Live-Action)  
**Genre:** Coming-of-age / Teen Drama / Social Satire  
**Creator / Director / Producer:** Mark Majcherczyk-Olczak  
**Production Company:** Majcherczyk Digital Ltd  

---

## 🧠 Logline

Tessa Reed, a quiet and artistic teenager, is pushed into the spotlight when she is mistaken for a missing influencer — forcing her to question her identity in a world obsessed with online attention.

---

## 📖 Synopsis

#NOTHER follows Tessa Reed, a thoughtful and creative teenager who feels disconnected from a world dominated by phones, followers, and constant online validation.

While her best friend Maya is driven by a desire for internet fame, Tessa prefers drawing, reading, and the analogue side of life.

Everything changes when a makeover causes Tessa to resemble a missing influencer known as Tess R. After a photo is accidentally posted online, she is suddenly mistaken for the famous creator.

Overnight, Tessa is thrust into a life of virality, public attention, and pressure to maintain an identity that is not her own.

As her online popularity grows, she must decide between fame and authenticity — ultimately discovering that her value lies in being herself rather than being admired by others.

## 🎬 Tone & Style

#NOTHER blends heartfelt coming-of-age storytelling with satirical observations of modern internet culture.

The tone balances:
- warmth and sincerity  
- humour and awkwardness  
- emotional pressure and introspection  

The film is designed to feel:
- character-driven  
- visually contemporary  
- emotionally accessible to teen and young adult audiences  

---

## 🎨 Visual Approach

The film’s visual language contrasts two worlds:

### Tessa’s World (Authenticity)
- Warm, natural lighting  
- Softer compositions  
- Calm, intimate framing  
- Grounded, analogue atmosphere  

### Social Media World (Virality)
- Brighter, harsher lighting  
- Graphic overlays and screens  
- Faster pacing and energy  
- Busier, high-contrast visuals  

As the story progresses, the visual style shifts as Tessa is pulled deeper into online fame, before returning to a grounded aesthetic.

Majcherczyk Digital Business Transfer Blueprint

GitHub Transfer Instructions

Go to Settings → Account → Store Settings.
Invite the new owner as a staff member or admin.
Transfer ownership once the buyer accepts the invite.
Remove yourself from the store if desired.

Namecheap Transfer Instructions

Log in to your Namecheap account.
Go to "Manage" for the domain you want to transfer.
Click on "Transfer" and follow the prompts.
Send the transfer code to the new owner.

Shopify Transfer Instructions

Go to Settings → Account → Store Settings.
Invite the new owner as a staff member or admin.
Transfer ownership once the buyer accepts the invite.
Remove yourself from the store if desired.

Printify Transfer Instructions

Go to Printify → Account Settings → Store Management.
Disconnect your store from Printify.
Invite the new store owner to connect their Printify account.
Update the billing information if necessary.

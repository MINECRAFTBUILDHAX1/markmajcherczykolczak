# VFX & Compositing – Mark Majcherczyk-Olczak

Official page: https://markmajcherczykolczak.com/vfx

Mark Majcherczyk-Olczak is a visual effects artist specialising in compositing using Nuke, with a focus on integrating live-action footage and digital elements to achieve clean, cinematic results.

---

## 🎬 Overview

Mark works primarily in **node-based compositing**, developing shots from raw plates through to final delivery with attention to realism, colour, and integration.

His work focuses on:
- seamless visual integration  
- natural-looking composites  
- storytelling through visual effects  

---

## 🧠 Specialisation

**Primary Skill:** Compositing using Nuke

## 🎥 Workflow Approach

Mark follows a structured compositing workflow:

1. Plate preparation and analysis  
2. Keying / roto work  
3. Layer integration (foreground, midground, background)  
4. Colour matching and light consistency  
5. Final grading and refinement
